J. Robert Oppenheimer

Father of the atomic bomb

Here's a time line of Oppenheimer life :

  • 1904 -Born in New York

  • 1922 Starts Harvard University , initially wants to study chemistry, but prefers physics

  • 1925 Completes four years of education in three years and graduated with summa cum laude (highest honors)

  • 1927 Receives his doctorate in 1927 at the age of 22. In Göttingen Universty, he publishes many articles on quantum theory in particular.

  • 1940 Marries with Katherine (Kitty) Harrison

  • 1941 -Upon reports that Germany was developing an atomic bomb, US President Franklin D. Roosevelt initiates the Manhattan Project in 1941.

  • 1942 -Although it is known that Oppenheimer's involvement with communism and that his brother and sister-in-law are members of the Communist Party, he is brought in charge of the project a year later..
  • 1945 -Assembles a team of qualified scientists and establishes a research center in Los Alamos (New Mexico). On July 16, 1945, Oppenheimer and his team witnessed the first atomic explosion (known as the Trinity Experiment). The explosion had the equivalent of an explosion of 18,000 tons of TNT. “We were conscious that the world would not go on as before,” said Oppenheimer for the moment.

  • 1945 -after a month American planes drop two atomic bombs on the Japanese cities of Hiroshima and Nagasaki. More than 140,000 people die. Within a week, Japan surrenders to the Allies. World War II ends.

  • 1947-1952 -He is appointed head of the US Atomic Energy Advisory Committee.

  • 1967 -Oppenheimer was a lifelong heavy smoker and died of throat cancer on February 18, 1967, at the age of 62..
